Privacy & Security - EveryDayEase

Your Privacy Matters to Us

At EveryDayEase, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring secure service transactions.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, phone number, email, and address for service bookings.
  • Payment Information: Securely processed through trusted payment gateways.
  • Location Data: Used to find nearby service providers (only if permitted).
  • Usage Data: Helps improve our services and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To facilitate service bookings and communication.
  • To improve user experience through personalized recommendations.
  • To process payments securely.
  • To provide customer support and resolve issues.

3. Data Protection Measures

  • SSL Encryption: Ensures secure transactions.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel handle your data.
  • Security Audits: Regular updates to maintain privacy.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Verified service providers for bookings.
  • Payment processors for transactions.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access & Update: Modify your personal details in your account.
  • Delete Account: Request data removal.
  • Control Notifications: Manage alerts and marketing emails.

For privacy concerns, contact us at support@everydayease.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve website performance, store preferences, and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
